# Expenses-dashboard-power-bi
# 💸 Personal Finance & Lifestyle Dashboard

## 🧠 Overview

Money tells a story — not just of numbers, but of choices, habits, and priorities.

This Power BI dashboard dives deep into an individual's financial lifestyle, tracking how income flows through daily life — from essential spending to indulgences — and ultimately, how much is truly being saved.

It’s not just about *where the money goes*, but whether those patterns are building a better future… or quietly breaking it.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze and visualize:

* Income vs Expenses
* Spending behavior across lifestyle categories
* Savings trends over time
* Financial discipline and growth patterns

---

## 📊 Key Features

### 💰 Income vs Expenses

* Clear comparison of earnings and total expenditure
* Monthly breakdown to identify surplus or deficit

### 🍔 Category-wise Spending

* Food 🍕
* Travel ✈️
* Entertainment 🎬
* Other lifestyle expenses

→ Understand *where money actually disappears*

### 📈 Savings Analysis

* Monthly savings tracking
* Savings growth trend over time
* Identification of good vs bad financial months

### 🔍 Behavioral Insights

* Spending patterns and habits
* High-expense triggers
* Lifestyle vs savings trade-off

## 💡 Key Insights

* Consistent high spending on lifestyle categories can significantly impact savings
* Small, repeated expenses often contribute more to financial drain than one-time big purchases
* Positive savings trends are directly linked to controlled discretionary spending
* Financial discipline is visible through stable expense patterns over time
